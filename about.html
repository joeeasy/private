Our Story
HauzRent was inspired through the painpoint experienced by seeing young professionals attempting to pay rentbills. Most professionals working in large firms kept requesting for advances while others sought for loans with ridiculous interest rates. Discipline should be rewarded. To save for a cause like rents payment, it should be encouraged as well as rewarding. Rent payment will always be a recurring bill. There should be a solution and HauzRent is poised to eliminate the bottle necks that makes it difficult for the working class individuals to live in major cities across Nigeria.

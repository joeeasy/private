What is HauzRent?
HauzRent is an online savings platform that allows tenants pay rents with a specified savings plan with the use of their ATM/Debitcards while enjoying rewards.Users have the opportunity for their rents being paid for them before the landlord comes knocking and other benefits.
How do I get started on HauzRent?
  • Create an account on HauzRent with your name, email address and mobile number.
  • Confirm your details which will be sent to your email address.
  • Select a savings plan and insert your card details.
  • Choose an automatic savings date.
  • Watch HauzRent grow your money.
What is the benefits of using HauzRent?

We know you love to focus on what's important and not worry. We are here to:

  • Allow you earn interests higher than what the banks offer
  • Avoid uncomfortable discussions with loved ones regarding rents.
  • Allow you focus on work that's productive.
  • Help you improve your financial strength by providing discipline

When are the funds deduted from my account?
Once you are signedup and select an Automatic savings plan, the funds will be deducted for rent from your account on the specified date. You will receive notifications via text and email on all your deductions.
What is the intrest rate for saving with HauzRent?
We have an unbeatable offer for you. If you save for 11months, we will pay the 12th month for you. You get interests up to 15% based on your monthly contribution
What if my account is not funded?
Unfortunately, we will be unable to help you reach your goal. This will affect your credit worthiness that qualifies you to apply for a loan. However, you could make use of another card to make a deposite
When can I withdraw my savings?
Withdrawing your funds is very easy. From your account, you can request for withdrawal. However, withdrawing before the set day will bring about a fine. We do this to encourage you to be disciplined.
When can I withdraw my savings?
There is no limit to your savings plan as long as your funds are available on the set date for deposite.
